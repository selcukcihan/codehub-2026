## What is Cloud Computing?

Cloud is just someone else's computer.
It's someone else's fault tolerant, highly available, backed up, geographically distributed set of computers continuously monitored and updated.
Managed by an excellent team of engineers and available for you to use, billed per second or even millisecond resolution.

Private vs. Public Cloud

## Cloud Providers

- Amazon AWS, Google GCP, Microsoft Azure, Oracle, Cloudflare, Netlify, Digital Ocean, IBM

## Serverless

Serverless is a mindset: you do not manage any servers, you do not patch, scale or replace servers.
The more you can offload to the provider, the better.

There's also the notion of vendor lock-in. When you offload too much to the cloud provider, people think it may lock them in with that provider. This is not a valid concern, especially in the AI era.
